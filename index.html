About me

Where it all started ...

It was a hot summer's evening and I was supposed to be studying for an exam. I was in Grade 8 but I remember it like it was yesterday. I was paging through a Huisgenoot and came across a picture of Brad Pitt sitting on a motorbike. Something tickled in my brain and I grabbed a pencil and a piece of paper.

Not even a blank paper, the one one with lines across that I tore out the book I was supposed to be making notes in. I started drawing and a coupled of minutes later I stared at the paper, surprised that I got more than a stick drawing out of it but a picture that kind of resembled Brad Pitt. And as they say, the rest is history.
